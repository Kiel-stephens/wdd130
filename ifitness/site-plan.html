Overview

Purpose

iFitness Hub is a health, recreational, and social facility geared towards exercise, sports, and other physical activities. We are here to help you learn new exercises, techniques and training methods to meet your weight-loss, muscle building, heart health or sports performance goals while making sure you use the best and up-to-date equipment.

Audience

Our program suits everyone and every age range. Do you feel you are overweight and need to shed some pounds, are you looking to replace those fats with some muscles, are you finding it difficult to find consistency in your daily routines? You are right to be here.

Classes

We have designed classes to meet your fitness needs, and each class is capped at 20 people so everyone can get the individual attention they need. All classes are first-come, first-served, so please show up 10-15 minutes early. If a class gets full, you may have to register for a private session at a slightly more cost. Whichever class type you attend is tailored to meet your body goals.
