Classes

We have designed classes to meet your fitness needs, and each class is capped at 20 people so everyone can get the individual attention they need. All classes are first-come, first-served, so please show up 10-15 minutes early. If a class gets full, you may have to register for a private session at a slightly more cost. Whichever class type you attend is tailored to meet your body goals.

Weightlifting

In this advanced series you'll determine your weight class and learn how to properly do a deadlift, squat, and bench press.

Yoga

Open to any level of practice, our Yoga class is all about syncing our movements with our breath and linking each pose to create a seamless sequence.

Basic Strength Training

Here, we combine free weights with squats, lunges, and high-interval exercises so that you can build strength according to your own body type and goals.

Cardio

Welcome to your perfect lunch break workout. During this short class, we do a series of intense cross-training moves with bursts of aerobic exercise, weight lifting, and stretch breaks.

Gymnastics

For adult beginners and intermediates alike, this class will teach you the basics of gymnastics and help you build the strength you need to hold proper balance.

Guided Nutrition

To achieve your desired body, what you eat is as important as what you do. Here our nutritionist will help you with intermittent fastings and a meal plan for each day.
